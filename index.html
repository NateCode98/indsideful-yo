Why Insideful?

The top reason why ideas fail is because someone build something that no one needs.

We all know - rule number one of creating a success project - is to go out and talk to your users. Cool! This is what most of us do - but then, how do you keep track of your gained insights after you took a picture of your user data? How often are you really reviewing your hypothesis? How do you make a decision, when there is basically no data ? How do you communicate this decision with your colleagues?

If you don't have an answer to all of these questions - don't worry Insideful will help from now on.

Outstanding features

Link specific findings to assumptions and rate them

Go out to your users and talk to them. After taking notes, highlight the parts that have an impact on your assumptions and rate this impact. It works like leave a comment within a Google Docs - very straight forward.

Know when to make decisions.

Have always an overview of your assumption. See which parts are linked to them. Be ready to make decisions and get your colleagues a reason for your decisions.
